Markup Purpose NVDA Jaws 2020 VoiceOver macOS
aria-details Provide a reference to additional visible information on the page. The screen would announce the availability of additional information and provide a means to navigate tot he descriptive content.

NOTE: This feature was added at the request of DPUB working group, so may have better support in digital book readers.

 None
  • Announces additional information available.
  • Announces when entering or leaving a details container, similar to landmarks.
  • Does NOT provide information relationships between references and containers.
  • Does not provide a means to navigate between references and details containers.
